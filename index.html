About Our Program

Web Coding and More is a comprehensive tutorial designed for high school students. Our program covers a wide range of topics, including web development, game design, robotics, and artificial intelligence, all integrated with Christian values and principles.

Our Values

  • Family: We encourage strong family bonds and community support.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our educational endeavors.
